Roast Chicken Thighs

Ah yes! Yummy yummy proteins!

Juicy and tender cut of dark meat received from slaying a chicken.

Drop rate of a roast chicken thigh is about 50% when slaying a chicken but increases up to 70% if slayed with a fire based attack.

Alternatively, roast chicken thighs are also obtainable by roasting a chicken!

Ingredients

  • Live chicken
  • A microphone
  • An audience to witness the roast
  • At least Roasting skill 3

Steps

  1. Bring the live chicken on stage where visible to audience
  2. Start roasting it's mother
  3. If successful, roast chicken thighs should drop within minutes
  4. If unsuccessful, please reattempt after leveling roast skill
